About us

Just a couple of friends...


We are obsessed with exploring the wild and discovering the beauty of the natural world. From mountain hikes to beach walks, we are constantly seeking out new adventures that allow us connect with nature and learn about the environment. We are always looking for new spots or trails without light pollution and absolutely amazing night sky views. We bring you peace and relax when city life is too much overwhelming.


...looking for spots without the light pollution.

Light pollution has a range of negative impacts on humans, including disruption of circadian rhythms, interference with astronomical observation, and decreased visibility at night. Light pollution can lead to sleep disorders, depression, and other health issues. It also affects wildlife, disrupting the natural life cycles of animals that rely on natural light cycles to survive. Additionally, light pollution wastes energy, leading to higher costs for electricity and contributing to the global climate crisis.
